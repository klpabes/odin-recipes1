Pancit Canton

Boiled instant noodles

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Pour instant noodles into a pot of boiling water in 200ml
  2. Stir the instant noodles until soft
  3. Pour the pot of boiling water with soft instant noodles into a strainer to remove water content
  4. Pour noodles into a plate or bowl
  5. Pour soy sauce, oil and seasoning into the cooked instant noodles
  6. Enjoy!
